Simple Pasta Carbonara

A quick and delicious Italian pasta dish.

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
- Black pepper to taste
- Salt for pasta water

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
2. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente
3. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large pan until crispy
4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheese, and plenty of black pepper
5. Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain pasta
6. Remove pan from heat and add hot pasta to pancetta
7. Quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water to create a creamy sauce
8. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes
Serves 4 people

This is a classic Roman dish that's ready in under 30 minutes!
