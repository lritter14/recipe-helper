Quick Avocado Toast

A simple and healthy breakfast option.

Ingredients:
- 2 slices whole grain bread
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, poached egg

Directions:
1. Toast the bread until golden brown
2. While bread is toasting, mash avocado in a bowl
3. Mix in lemon juice, salt, and pepper
4. Spread avocado mixture evenly on toasted bread
5. Top with cherry tomatoes or other toppings if desired
6. Serve immediately

Takes about 5 minutes to prepare.
Serves 2 people.

Perfect for a quick breakfast or snack!
